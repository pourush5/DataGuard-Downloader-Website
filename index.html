Supercharge Your Downloads

Packed with utility features designed specifically to safeguard your daily data pack.

Smart Budget Guard

Set your daily limit (e.g., 1.5GB) and downloads will automatically pause the split second you reach it, protecting you from overage costs.

Resumable Engine

Stop, pause, or resume downloads at will. DataGuard remembers every single byte downloaded so you never waste bandwidth redownloading.

Cookie Syncing

Our companion browser extension carries over your cookies, allowing you to seamlessly download files from authenticated sites.

Link Refresher

Expired download link? Refresh it instantly inside the app to resume the download from where it was paused without losing progress.

Self-Healing Ports

Automatically scans and binds to free local ports if standard ports are busy, ensuring your app launches perfectly every time.

Bandwidth Analytics

Monitor real-time speed charts and historical daily consumption tables to understand your download behavior over time.
